About Strategic Commodities Controls

Under the Import and Export (Strategic Commodities) Ordinance (Cap. 60), certain goods require a licence from the Trade and Industry Department before they can be imported, exported, or trans-shipped through Hong Kong. This includes dual-use items, military goods, and other controlled technologies. This screening tool provides an initial keyword-based check — it does not constitute legal advice. Always consult the official Strategic Commodities Control List for definitive classification.
