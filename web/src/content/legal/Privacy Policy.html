Privacy Policy

1. Introduction and Territorial Scope

1.1 This Privacy Policy explains how Sentimento Technologies Limited (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit www.monoclaw.app (“Website”) or use the Dashboard.

1.2 Global Access, HK-Only Service: While the Website is accessible worldwide, we only provide services to HK SAR residents/entities. For non-HK visitors, we process only technical data necessary for website security and export control screening. For HK clients, we process data as detailed below under the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) (“PDPO”).

1.3 Data Roles:

·     Data User (Controller): For website analytics, hardware diagnostic logs, device photographs, contract execution data (signatures, IP addresses, audit trails), and business records.

·     Data Processor: For Client Account creation data (usernames, encrypted passwords) processed on behalf of our clients (the Data Users of their employee data).

 

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Authentication Data: Google SSO tokens (sub claim), email verification status, session data. We do not store Google passwords.

2.2 Identity Verification Data:

·     For individuals: HKID number (last 4 digits only), full name, Hong Kong residential address;

·     For entities: Business Registration Certificate number, company name, registered HK address, beneficial ownership information (for sanctions screening).

2.3 Transaction Data: Apple Order Confirmation Numbers, hardware serial numbers, MAC addresses, model specifications, Stripe transaction IDs. We do not store credit card numbers (processed by Stripe).

2.4 Technical Data: IP addresses, device fingerprints (browser type, OS, screen resolution), timestamps (HKST, NTP-synchronised), session duration, geolocation data (for export control compliance).

2.5 Professional Credentials: For Tier 1 Tools (Legal, Medical, Accounting, Immigration): practising certificates, professional indemnity insurance certificates, supervisory confirmations (Clause 4.6, Master Agreement).

2.6 Diagnostic Data: Hardware diagnostic logs and photographic evidence of device condition/seal application (stored via Supabase in Singapore).

 

 

 

3. Legal Basis for Processing

3.1 HK Clients (PDPO):

·     Performance of Contract: To execute the Master Agreement and provide Configuration Services;

·     Legal Compliance: To comply with the Gatekeeper Protocol, export controls (Cap. 60), and sanctions screening;

·     Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention, network security, and enforcement of our HK-only service restrictions.

3.2 Non-HK Visitors (GDPR Article 49 Derogations): For EU/EEA/UK visitors browsing the site but ineligible for services, we rely on:

·     Article 49(1)(d): Processing necessary for important reasons of public interest (export control compliance and sanctions prevention);

·     Article 49(1)(b): Processing necessary for the performance of a contract (if you proceed to sign up and verify HK eligibility).

3.3 CCPA (California): We do not “sell” personal information. We process minimal data for security and compliance only.

 

4. Use of Data

·     Service Delivery: Authenticating Dashboard access, processing orders, executing electronic signatures via our Native Signing System (Clause 1.3, Master Agreement);

·     Export Control Compliance: Screening against OFAC SDN List, EU Consolidated List, UN Sanctions to prevent prohibited users from accessing dual-use AI technology;

·     Security & Audit: Maintaining immutable audit trails (SHA-256 hashing, WORM storage) for non-repudiation (Clause 1.5);

·     Professional Verification: Verifying credentials for Tier 1 Tools (retained for duration of Agreement + 6 years per Clause 4.6);

·     Communication: Posting notices exclusively to the Dashboard (Clause 11.6).

6. Data Retention

·     Hardware logs/photographs: 1 year from delivery date, then permanent deletion (Clause 6.3(a));

·     Contract execution data (signatures, audit trails): 7 years from execution (or longer if required by limitation periods) (Schedule C, Section 8(b));

·     Account data: Deleted within 30 days of contract termination, unless legal proceedings require retention;

·     Sanctions screening records: 5 years minimum (regulatory requirement);

·     Failed registration attempts (non-HK): 90 days then deletion.

 

7. Your Rights

7.1 PDPO Rights (HK): You may request access to and correction of your personal data. A reasonable fee may be charged for access requests.

7.2 GDPR Rights (EU Visitors): Subject to Article 49 derogations, you may request erasure of browsing data. You have no right to data portability for service data as we cannot provide services outside HK.

7.3 CCPA Rights (California): You may request disclosure of categories of personal information collected (minimal) and request deletion (subject to sanctions screening retention).

7.4 Exercising Rights: Submit requests via the Dashboard secure messaging system. We respond within 30 days.

 

8. Security Measures

·     Encryption: Passwords hashed using bcrypt/Argon2; TLS 1.3 for transmission; AES-256 for data at rest;

·     Access Controls: Role-based access limited to essential personnel;

·     Audit Trails: Immutable WORM storage for signed contracts and audit logs (Clause 1.5);

·     Geoblocking: IP-based restrictions for sanctioned territories.

9. Third-Party Processors

·     Google LLC: SSO authentication (subject to Google Privacy Policy);

·     Stripe Inc.: Payment processing (subject to Stripe Privacy Policy);

·     Supabase Inc.: Database hosting (subject to Supabase Privacy Policy).

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices (Clause 7.2, Master Agreement).

 

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy by posting changes to the Dashboard. Material changes regarding data use will be notified via Dashboard urgent notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
