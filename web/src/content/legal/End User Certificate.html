3. END-USE ASSURANCES

3.1 Permitted Uses: Client undertakes that the Local Models will be used exclusively for:

·     (a) Civilian commercial, professional, or academic research applications as specified in the Order Form;

·     (b) Internal business operations within Client’s organization;

·     (c) Administrative, analytical, or automation tasks consistent with any Vertical Modules selected under Schedule B of the Master Agreement.

3.2 Prohibited Uses: Client specifically covenants that the Local Models will NOT be used, directly or indirectly, for:

·     (a) Military End-Uses: Development, production, operation, installation, maintenance, repair, overhaul, or refurbishing of military items (as defined in EAR §744.17), including weapons, weapons delivery systems, unmanned aerial vehicles, or intelligence applications for military end-users;

·     (b) Surveillance: Mass surveillance, facial recognition for law enforcement, social scoring systems, or data analytics for intelligence agencies or internal security forces;

·     (c) Weapons of Mass Destruction: Design, development, production, or use of nuclear, chemical, or biological weapons, or missile technology;

·     (d) Human Rights Violations: Systematic discrimination, torture, or other cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment prohibited under international human rights law;

·     (e) Prohibited AI Applications:

o   Generating deceptive synthetic media for fraud, scams, or political manipulation (Meta Llama prohibition);

o   Automated medical diagnosis or treatment decisions without qualified professional supervision (Google Gemma prohibition);

o   Generation of unlawful sexually explicit content involving minors (Google Gemma prohibition);

o   Weapons development or military/naval applications (Alibaba Cloud Qwen prohibition).

 

4. RE-EXPORT AND TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

4.1 Export Control Classification: Client acknowledges that:

·     (a) The Local Models specified in Schedule A may constitute Dual-Use Goods under Category 3 of the Wassenaar Arrangement and may be controlled under ECCN 3A001 or 3A090 of the U.S. Commerce Control List;

·     (b) Sentimento holds no export licence for deployment outside the Authorized Jurisdiction;

·     (c) Download of model weights from repositories hosted in the People’s Republic of China (DeepSeek, Zhipu, Alibaba) or United States (Meta, Google, Mistral) to Hong Kong is authorised only for end-use within the Authorized Jurisdiction.

4.2 Absolute Prohibition on Re-export: Without prior written consent of Sentimento and valid export licences from the Hong Kong Trade and Industry Department (TID) and/or U.S. Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), Client shall NOT:

·     (a) Export, re-export (as defined in EAR §734.14), or transfer (in-country) the Client Hardware containing Local Models to any destination outside the Authorized Jurisdiction;

·     (b) Disclose or transfer model weights, parameter files, or technical data to any national of a comprehensively sanctioned country, even if located in Hong Kong (deemed export restriction);

·     (c) Allow remote access to the Local Models by persons located outside Hong Kong where such access would constitute a deemed export under EAR §734.14.

4.3 Relocation Notification: If Client intends to relocate Client Hardware containing Local Models outside Hong Kong for any reason (including business relocation, personal travel, or sale), Client must:

·     (a) Notify Sentimento in writing at least thirty (30) days prior to relocation;

·     (b) Obtain independent legal advice regarding export licensing requirements of the destination jurisdiction;

·     (c) Secure all necessary export/import licences at Client’s sole cost and expense;

·     (d) Provide Sentimento with evidence of such licences prior to relocation;

·     (e) If relocation is to a sanctioned jurisdiction, Client acknowledges Sentimento must report the transaction to relevant authorities.

 

5. UPSTREAM LICENSOR COMPLIANCE

Client specifically agrees to comply with the following Third-Party Terms as a condition of using the respective Local Models:

5.1 Meta Platforms, Inc. (Llama 3.x): Compliance with Llama 3 Community Licence prohibitions on surveillance, military use, and human rights violations; monthly active user reporting if exceeding 700 million users.

5.2 Alibaba Cloud (Qwen): Compliance with Qwen Licence export control restrictions and prohibition on weapons/military use.

5.3 Google LLC (Gemma): Compliance with Gemma Terms of Use prohibitions on harmful deepfakes, unlawful content, and medical automation.

5.4 DeepSeek AI / Zhipu AI: Compliance with MIT Licence attribution requirements and PRC export control regulations.

5.5 Mistral AI: Compliance with Apache 2.0 licence terms where applicable.

Client acknowledges that breach of Upstream Licensor terms may result in termination of Client’s licence to use the models and indemnification obligations under Clause 8 of the Master Agreement.

·     Verification of Business Registration status;

·     Review of access logs to confirm hardware has not been removed from Hong Kong.

6.3 Client consents to Sentimento screening Client’s name and beneficial owners against Sanctions Lists prior to deployment and periodically thereafter using third-party compliance software.
